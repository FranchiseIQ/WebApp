How to Use

Adding Stocks

  1. Enter a ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL)
  2. Click "Add" or press Enter
  3. You can add up to 10 tickers at once

Default Stocks

SPY (Black): S&P 500 ETF as market benchmark

9 Franchise Stocks: MCD, YUM, QSR, WEN, DPZ, JACK, WING, SHAK, DENN

Chart Controls

  • Zoom: Scroll or pinch to zoom
  • Pan: Click and drag
  • Reset: Click "Reset" button
